Why track your heart rate?

Your heart is the machine that powers your body. It is amazingly efficient and powerful, but it will only beat so many times before it gives out.

If you can lower your heart rate by 1 Beat Per Minute, you will save nearly 525,600 heart beats in 1 year. At an average heart rate of 70 Beats Per Minute, that's 5 heart days of life.

Imagine lowering your heart rate by 5 Beats Per minute. You will save nearly 2,628,000 heart beats in 1 year. That's 26 heart days of life, almost 1 month!
